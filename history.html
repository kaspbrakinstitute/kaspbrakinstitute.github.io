History

Our mission


The Kaspbrak Institute was founded by Dr. Edward Kaspbrak in 2010 to promote a culture of truth. Our team of experts works to investigate claims of the paranormal and use reasoning to demystify the seemingly unexplainable, fostering a healthy sense of skepticism to the approach of paranormal research and placing faith in known scientific methods to bring us closer to understanding the world in which we live. Skepticism and scientific reasoning is at the heart of what we do. We seek to understand the extraordinary. Above all else, we value truth, and hope to inspire others to pursue the truths hidden in claims of the supernatural.




Timeline

2010: Kaspbrak Institute is founded by Dr. Kaspbrak.
2011: Dr. Kaspbrak makes waves in the field of paranormal research when he demystifies the case of the Chatham haunting.
2012: The Kaspbrak Institute announces its million dollar prize
2015: Following explosive growth, the Kaspbrak Institute expands its team to allow for faster turnaround in demystified cases.
Present: Dr. Kaspbrak regularly appears as a guest lecturer, op-ed writer, and panelist.
